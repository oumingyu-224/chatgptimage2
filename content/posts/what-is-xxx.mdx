ChatGPT Image 2 is an AI image generation and editing platform designed for practical visual creation. It is especially useful for users who need polished results for posters, ecommerce assets, product imagery, social graphics, and reference-guided edits.

## What makes ChatGPT Image 2 useful?

ChatGPT Image 2 is positioned around a set of practical strengths:

- High-quality image generation
- Image editing support
- Stronger prompt adherence
- Better reference image fidelity
- Text rendering inside images
- Transparent background output

That combination makes it suitable for workflows that go beyond simple text-to-image experimentation.

## Common use cases

Teams and creators typically look for ChatGPT Image 2 when they need:

1. Marketing posters and social graphics
2. Ecommerce hero images and product visuals
3. Reference-based image edits and restyling
4. Sticker-style assets with transparent backgrounds
5. Images that include readable text elements

## Prompting tips

To get better results, write prompts that clearly describe:

- The subject
- The composition
- The lighting
- The materials or textures
- Any text that should appear in the image
- Whether you want a transparent background
- What should be preserved from a reference image

## Workflow suggestions

If you are creating from scratch, start with a detailed text prompt.

If you need higher consistency or brand control, upload reference images and specify what must stay unchanged. This is especially useful for products, packaging, character details, and layout preservation.

## Quality expectations

ChatGPT Image 2 is better understood as quality-first rather than speed-first. More complex prompts and image edits may take longer, but the tradeoff is better alignment for commercial or presentation-ready visual output.
